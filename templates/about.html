About the Product

The EDA Visualizer is a powerful tool designed to help users explore and analyze their datasets in a visually intuitive way. The main objective is to provide an in-depth analysis of the dataset. The focus are on three aspects: Data Visualization, Statistical Analysis and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA). The product will offer a comprehensive tools and features to help users understand their data and make informed decisions based on the insights gained from the analysis.
